Course Building Time!

It's time to design your course. Layout all connected phones on your table to form your very own minigolf course.

To mark a connection, use two fingers and slide across the edge on both devices. If successful, you'll see a blue line appearing indicating the connection. You must draw the connections one after the other (0 to 1, then 1 to 2, etc).
